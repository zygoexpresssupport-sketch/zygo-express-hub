FAQ

Questions about delivery
in Wa, Ghana.

How much does delivery cost in Wa, Ghana? +
Zygo Express charges based on road distance. Deliveries within 2km cost GHS 13. Up to 4km is GHS 16. Up to 6km is GHS 20. Deliveries up to 9km cost GHS 30. The exact price is shown to you automatically before you confirm your booking at zygoexpress.com.
How long does delivery take in Wa? +
Our average pickup time in Wa Municipal is 28 minutes from when you confirm your order. Most deliveries within Wa are completed within 1–3 hours. We operate from 7am to 9pm, 7 days a week including Sundays.
Can I track my delivery in Wa? +
Yes. Every Zygo Express delivery in Wa comes with a unique tracking code (format: ZGX-XXXXXX). You can track your parcel's status in real time at zygoexpress.com. You can also see when it has been picked up and when it has been delivered, with GPS-confirmed proof of delivery.
How do I pay for delivery in Wa? +
We accept MTN Mobile Money (MoMo) as our primary payment method. After booking, you send payment to 0559382734(Zygo Express) using your tracking ID as the reference. Dial *170# on your MTN line to make the payment.
Can Zygo Express collect parcels from STC or VIP bus terminals in Wa? +
Yes — this is one of our most popular services. If you have a parcel arriving at the STC terminal, VIP Jeoun terminal, or OA Travel & Tours in Wa, we can collect it and deliver it directly to your door. Just book with the terminal as your pickup location and your home address as drop-off.
Do you deliver medicine and prescriptions in Wa? +
Yes. Zygo Express is the official delivery partner for Prime Meditrust in Wa. We deliver prescriptions, lab reports, and medical supplies discreetly and promptly across Wa Municipal. All medical deliveries are handled with full discretion by our verified riders.
Is Zygo Express available on weekends and Sundays in Wa? +
Yes — we operate 7 days a week from 7am to 9pm, including Sundays and public holidays. This makes us the most available delivery service in Wa. You can book any day of the week at zygoexpress.com or WhatsApp us on 0202858011.
Which areas of Wa do you deliver to? +
We deliver to all areas within Wa Municipal including Kpaguri, Jahan, SSNIT, Dobile, Nakori, Kperisi, Sing, Bamahu, Charia, Wa Central Market, Regional Hospital, Municipal Hospital, UDS Campus, Wa Polytechnic, Lowcost, RST, Zongo, Kunta, Magazine Road, Loho, Dokpong, Kumbehi, Tendamba and more.
